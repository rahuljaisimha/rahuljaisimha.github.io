Biography

Good Marks! is a new band from Austin, Texas, dedicating to playing their absolute best for you. They all met at UT Austin in the fall of 2013 and have seen been playing as many shows for as many people as they can. Drawing inspiration from British Punk and Garage Rock, Good Marks hopes that real music played with real energy can live on into the future.
